Instructions

Welcome to Cat Colonies! It's like Asteroids meets Pacman, kinda. Your goal, as spacecat, is to conquer all the planets. Here's how:

  1. Use SPACE to fire lasers at the planets and break their shields. Each planet's shield is indicated in RED).
  2. After three successful shots, the planet's shield breaks (the red border will get thinner). Now it's time to conquer the planet! You conquer an unshielded planet by navigating over it.
  3. But be careful: you get hit by a planet with a still-active shield, you lose.
