Instructions

Hey, SpaceCat! Capture the alien planets by breaking their shields (indicated by the planet's rings)!

Three shots break a planet's shield. But be careful: If you get hit by a shielded planet, you lose!

  1. UP/DOWN arrows: move forward/back
  2. LEFT/RIGHT arrows: rotate SpaceCat
  3. SPACE: fire lasers at the planets.
